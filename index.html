Learning made to last

Mycel Project aims to make long-term learning pleasurable and efficient.

From curiosity to knowledge

Mycel Project supports every step of learning, from capturing what sparks your interest, to understanding how it connects, to retaining it for the long run.

Grows everywhere

Mycel is built to fit wherever you think. Use Mycelium on any platform, or connect to Mycel directly into your favorite knowledge tool.

Open by design

Mycel and Mycelium are open-source under the AGPL license : free to use, free to inspect, and free to shape. Contribute to its development, self-host it, or extend it to fit your workflow.

The root

Mycel is the name of the backend powering everything: from scheduling and data collection to processing and sync. Wherever it runs, your knowledge spreads.

Import

Start by importing from various sources: web articles, videos, podcasts, books. You can then prioritize your imports based on how much each source matters to you.

Fragment

Extract the richest parts of your imports. Keep refining over time, fragment by fragment, at your own pace following what sparks your interest, until each piece of knowledge stands on its own.

Spore

Once a fragment is refined enough, turn it into a spore: a precise, self-contained unit of knowledge ready to take root. Mycel uses spaced repetition to make sure each one grows and holds, long after you first encountered it.

Bloom

As your spores take root, each review strengthens not just the knowledge itself, but the connections between ideas. What once felt out of reach becomes natural ground, harder concepts finding their footing in everything you've already built.
