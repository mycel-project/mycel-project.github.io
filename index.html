Learning made to last

Mycel Project is an open ecosystem for long-term learning: capture, process, and retain knowledge across all your tools.

From curiosity to knowledge

Mycel Project supports every step of learning: capture what sparks your interest, break it down to understand it, and retain it for the long run.

Grows everywhere

Built around an open API, Mycel fits wherever you think. Download Mycelium, a cross-platform app powered by Mycel, or connect Mycel directly into your favorite tools.

Open by design

Mycel and Mycelium are open-source: free to use, free to inspect, and free to shape. Self-host it and keep your data local, contribute to its development, or extend it to fit your workflow.

The Root Engine

Mycel is the foundation that standardizes the whole learning lifecycle. Wherever it runs, your knowledge spreads.

A unified workflow across tools

Import

Start by importing from various sources: web articles, videos, podcasts, ebooks, etc. Prioritize your imports based on how much each source and topic matters to you.

Fragment

Break your imports down into their most valuable insights. Shape them until every single fragment becomes an independent unit of knowledge.

Spore

Turn refined fragments into spores: standalone, active learning units. Mycel uses spaced repetition to ensure you review them right before you forget.

Bloom

Each review strengthens the knowledge itself and the connections between your ideas. Harder concepts find their footing in everything you've already built.
